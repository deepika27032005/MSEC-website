About MEPCO SCHLENK ENGINEERING COLLEGE

Mepco Schlenk Engineering College (often called Mepco College, Sivakasi) is a well-known private engineering institution in Tamil Nadu, India. It was established on 17 October 1984 and is located near Sivakasi in Virudhunagar district.

Key points about the college:

- Established in 1984

- Located near Sivakasi, Virudhunagar district

- Private engineering college

- Affiliated with Anna University

- Approved by AICTE

Mepco Schlenk Engineering College, Sivakasi is a well-established autonomous engineering institution in Tamil Nadu that was founded in 1984 and is affiliated with Anna University, Chennai.
It is approved by AICTE and accredited with an A grade by NAAC and NBA Tier-I accreditation for most of its undergraduate and postgraduate programs, reflecting recognized academic standards and quality technical education.
The college offers a wide range of programs including B.E./B.Tech, M.E./M.Tech, MBA, MCA and other professional courses, with admissions to engineering programs typically through state counseling based on ranks.
It emphasizes Outcome Based Education, internships, research activity and industry-aligned skill development, and has multiple research centers, professional societies, and value-added courses to support overall student growth.
